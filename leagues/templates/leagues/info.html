Information about the ranking

A brief explanation of the ranking scores:

The score of a team is calculated by taking the average of the scores of the individual players in the team.

The scores are in logarithmic scale: A score difference of 10 means 2:1 ratio in the probabilities of winning points in a match. To give a few examples, score differences map to ratios of point winning probabilities as follows:

  • 1 ⇝ 15:14
  • 2 ⇝ 8:7
  • 3 ⇝ 5:4
  • 4 ⇝ 4:3
  • 6 ⇝ 3:2
  • 10 → 2:1
  • 16 ⇝ 3:1
  • 20 → 4:1
  • 26 ⇝ 6:1
  • 30 → 8:1
  • x → 2^(x/10)

Note that this works only when the number of players in both teams are equal.

Privacy

Although the pages are public there is a bit of privacy:

  • These web pages won't be found by search engines (e.g., Google).
  • There are no links to the pages of specific leagues on the front page.
Therefore, a league page can only be found by using a direct link given by someone who already has the link or by guessing different league IDs under the site domain and luckily finding a league site by chance. In addition, the player (or team) names can be chosen so that they do not reveal too much information about the identities if needed.
